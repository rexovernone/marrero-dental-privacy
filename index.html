Privacy Policy

Dr. Lois Marrero, DMD

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Information We Collect

We collect patient names and phone numbers solely for the purpose of scheduling dental appointments and sending appointment reminder text messages.

How We Use Your Information

Your phone number is used exclusively to send you appointment reminder text messages the day before your scheduled dental appointment. We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or promotional purposes.

Information Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties. Your data is used only for appointment reminder communications between our office and you.

Data Storage and Security

Your information is stored securely and is accessible only to authorized office staff. We take reasonable measures to protect your personal data from unauthorized access or disclosure.

Opt-Out

You may opt out of receiving appointment reminder text messages at any time by replying STOP to any message you receive from us, or by contacting our office directly.

Message Frequency and Charges

You will receive no more than one text message per scheduled appointment. Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this privacy policy or how your information is used, please contact our office directly.
