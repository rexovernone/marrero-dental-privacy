Dr. Lois Marrero, DMD — Appointment Reminder Program

Last updated: April 1, 2026

Program Name

Dr. Lois Marrero, DMD — Appointment Reminders

Program Description

By providing your phone number to our office, you consent to receive automated appointment reminder text messages from Dr. Lois Marrero, DMD. These messages are sent the day before your scheduled dental appointment to help you remember your visit.

Message Frequency

You will receive a maximum of one (1) text message per scheduled appointment.

Message and Data Rates

Message and data rates may apply depending on your mobile carrier and plan. Please contact your carrier for details about your messaging plan.

Opt-Out Instructions

You may opt out of receiving text messages at any time. To stop receiving messages, reply STOP to any message you receive from us. You will receive a confirmation message and will no longer receive appointment reminders via text.

Help and Support

For assistance or questions about this program, reply HELP to any message you receive from us, or contact our office directly.

Consent

Consent to receive text messages is not a condition of purchasing any goods or services. You may schedule and attend appointments without opting into text reminders.

Contact Us

If you have any questions about these terms, please contact our office directly.
